  • we love cooking Punjabi food.
  • we love eating Punjabi food.

  • we're just starting out, please bookmark the page and check back!.

How can you help?:

We cook a lot of our own food and we only know our local area. Email us with your recommendations for the best Punjabi Sikh restaurants in your area. We're particularly interested in the UK, GTA/Surrey in Canada, California, trucker dhabas in the US, the best dhabas on the route from Delhi to Punjab, etc. .

What We Believe:

  • Food is more than sustenance; it's a way to connect with others.
  • Supporting local businesses helps build stronger communities.
  • Everyone should have access to authentic Punjabi Sikh food compliant with the Sikh Rehat Maryada.
  • Enthusiasm matters! It's easier to find great food when you're passionate about exploration.
